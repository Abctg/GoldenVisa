10-Year Residency · Top Graduate Route

Your route to a UAE Golden Visa.

A start-to-finish plan built around your situation: an LSE graduate, an Indian passport with a US visa, and a goal of holding 10-year residency as a standing option — done online wherever the rules allow.

LSE · Bachelor's · 2026 Indian passport + US visa Abu Dhabi (ADRO) route ~2–3 months end to end
→

Why the Abu Dhabi route. It lets you submit the nomination from outside the UAE and get the eligibility decision before you travel — so you only fly in once you already know you're approved. The Dubai route (used in the guide you read) requires you to be physically present and apply on a gamble. Abu Dhabi is your primary path; Dubai is the fallback if ADRO isn't issuing this category when you apply.

PHASE 0

Set up before you graduate

Now → July · all online

Nothing here needs your degree in hand yet. Doing it now means you can move the moment LSE confers your award.

Create a UAE Pass account

This is the digital ID you'll log in with for the equivalency step and the nomination. Set it up early so it's ready.

Online

Check your US visa validity

Your US visa is what gets you UAE visa-on-arrival as an Indian citizen. Confirm it has at least 6 months left from your expected arrival date — and that your passport does too.

Pre-order LSE documents & warn the registry

Ask LSE registry now for the three documents you'll need, and tell them DataFlow will contact them to verify your graduation — this pre-empts the biggest delay in the whole process.

  • Official degree certificate
  • Official transcript with numerical marks
  • Official grading-scale statement (First = 70%+, etc.) — this is how you prove your result clears 3.5/4.0, since LSE won't issue a GPA

Confirm LSE is on the recognised list

Verify LSE appears on the current UAE Ministry of Education top-100 list (it qualifies via QS/THE, but check the live list).

PHASE 1

Verify & legalise your degree

July, once conferred · run both tracks at once

These two tracks are the slow part — so run them in parallel, not one after the other. This is where time is won or lost.

Track A — runs alongside Track B

MoHESR degree equivalency (via DataFlow)

Start on the MoHESR site; it redirects you to DataFlow. They contact LSE to confirm your graduation, then MoHESR issues the equivalency certificate — the UAE's own recognition of your degree. Budget several weeks; it's the slowest link, which is why you pre-warned the registry.

Mostly online ≈ £130 DataFlow ≈ £20 MoHESR
Track B — runs alongside Track A

Attest your documents (e-Apostille → digital embassy)

The fully-digital chain, in strict order:

  • Solicitor certification with a Qualified Electronic Signature — done online in a short video ID meeting
  • FCDO e-Apostille — digital, usually ~2 working days (no posting originals)
  • UAE Embassy London digital attestation — MOFA included, returned as a QR-coded PDF by email
Online ≈ £90–150 per document
Order matters: the UAE Embassy won't accept anything without the FCDO apostille first. And each document is processed separately — no bundling.
PHASE 2

Submit the nomination from home

Once Phase 1 is done · online

With the equivalency and attested documents in hand, you apply for the nomination from outside the UAE through ADRO / TAMM.

File the Golden Visa nomination (ADRO/TAMM)

Submit your academic documents and wait for pre-approval — typically a few weeks. Do this yourself rather than paying a centre to upload forms for you.

Online ≈ £30
The milestone you wanted

Once nomination is approved, you know you're in — before booking a single flight. Everything after this is procedural.

PHASE 3

One trip to the UAE

Only after pre-approval · in person

This is the only part that must be done on the ground, and your 14-day visa-on-arrival covers it comfortably. Do the medical, biometrics and Emirates ID together — the biometrics are tied to visa activation, so don't plan to skip them and return later.

Enter on visa-on-arrival

Your US visa makes you eligible. You can pre-arrange it online before flying rather than queuing at the airport counter.

≈ £25

Golden Visa application

Submit the formal application with your nomination approval. This is the single biggest fee.

≈ £575Pay cash

Medical fitness test

Blood test + chest X-ray. Smart Salem turns it around in ~30 minutes if you want speed; standard centres are cheaper if you're not rushing.

≈ £65–150

Biometrics + Emirates ID

Fingerprints, palms and your signature at a Customer Happiness Center (Al Ghazal or Al Nahda). You get a usable digital ID almost immediately; the physical card follows in days.

≈ £240
The timing trap: the Golden Visa biometrics counter runs 7 AM–3 PM only, one counter. Go at 7 AM. And take your time on the signature — it's printed permanently on the card.
Done

You hold 10-year, self-sponsored residency. You can leave and stay away indefinitely without losing it — it's yours as a standing option.

NOTE

About working

The visa is residency, not a work permit

The Golden Visa lets you live in the UAE and self-sponsor, but to take a salaried job you (via the employer) still need a MOHRE work permit. It's a quick, employer-driven step — about 5 working days, since your medical, Emirates ID and residence are already done — not a barrier. Running your own company or freelancing under the right setup doesn't need an employer's labour card. Never work on a visit visa.
